# PickMe Demo Script (3 minutes)

## Opening (20s)

"AI coding agents are making more technical decisions every day. They pick frameworks, choose APIs, select tools. But there's a problem: high quality tools are getting skipped because agents can't find them. There's no way to know if your tool is being picked or ignored. That's what PickMe solves."

## Discovery Tab (60s)

1. Type `https://fastapi.tiangolo.com` in the scan bar, hit Scan
2. While loading: "PickMe instantly scans your site the way an AI agent would."
3. Show the AI Visibility Score: "This is your discoverability score out of 100."
4. Scroll to Discovery Signals: "Each finding tells you exactly what's wrong and why it matters. For example, missing sitemap means ClaudeBot and GPTBot can't find your deep pages."
5. Scroll to Bot Access Map: "We check all 16 major AI bots against your robots.txt."
6. Scroll to Agent's View: "This is literally what an AI agent sees. No CSS, no JavaScript, just the raw content."

## Simulation Tab (90s)

1. Click Simulation tab
2. Type `Hono` in the tool name field
3. "Now the powerful part. I just type a tool name. PickMe auto-generates everything: a description, a task, and real competitors."
4. Hit Run Agent Simulation
5. Point to the session log streaming in: "Watch this. Two real AI agents are running right now. Agent A has the original description. Agent B has our optimized version. They're searching the web, reading documentation, comparing options."
6. Wait for results: "Look at the decisions. Agent A picked [X], Agent B picked [Y]. The optimization changed the outcome."
7. If both pick the same: "Both agents picked Hono, but look at the reasoning. Agent B's reasoning is more specific, citing the exact features our optimization highlighted."

## Closing (30s)

"PickMe is the first tool that lets you measure, optimize, and validate how discoverable your tool is to AI agents. We use Claude's actual production tool search mechanism for benchmarking. Every optimization is backed by research from the GEO paper and MCP best practices. This isn't SEO. This is a new surface that didn't exist before agents started making decisions."
